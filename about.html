About Dahawi Engineering

We plan, build, operate, and maintain electrical infrastructure that keeps energy moving safely and reliably.

built for

our engineering Story

Power Infrastructure Specialists

Engineering Support From Planning to Maintenance

Dahawi Engineering Company is an international company specialized in the execution, operation, and maintenance of energy and electrical infrastructure projects. The company operates in the construction of distribution substations, implementation of integrated electrical schemes, and maintenance of transmission and distribution network components, including towers, poles, transformers, cables, and medium-voltage switchgear. We combine technical discipline with practical field experience so every project is delivered with attention to safety, reliability, and long-term performance.

a message from the CEO

At Dahawi Engineering Co. Ltd we believe that robust energy infrastructure is the driving force behind economic advancement, industrial growth, and sustainable development. Our mission is to deliver world-class engineering solutions that enhance the reliability, efficiency, and resilience of the power systems. By combining technical expertise, innovation, and uncompromising quality standards, we provide safe and dependable services that meet the evolving needs of our clients and the industries we serve.

Driven by engineering excellence, we strengthen critical infrastructure, enable sustainable growth, and create lasting value through trusted partnerships and high-impact projects. Our vision is to advance modern, resilient, and sustainable energy systems that power progress across Africa and beyond.

As CEO, I believe strong leadership, skilled teams, and clear communication are the foundations of successful engineering delivery. We are here to build trust, solve complex challenges, and power the future with confidence.

— Eng. Zunoon Adam Dahawi, CEO
