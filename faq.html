Common Questions

What services does Dahawi Engineering provide?
We provide integrated solutions in power infrastructure, substations, transmission and distribution networks, renewable energy systems, industrial electrical services, engineering consultancy, and operation & maintenance services.
What industries do you serve?
We serve utilities, industrial facilities, commercial developments, government institutions, healthcare facilities, telecommunications, and infrastructure projects.
Why should I choose Dahawi Engineering?
Our team combines technical expertise, industry experience, quality-driven execution, and a commitment to delivering reliable, efficient, and sustainable energy solutions tailored to each client's needs.
How do you ensure quality and safety in your projects?
We implement strict quality assurance and health, safety, and environmental (QHSE) procedures throughout all project phases, from design and construction to commissioning and maintenance.
What certifications and standards do you comply with?
Our projects are executed in accordance with internationally recognized engineering, safety, and quality standards, as well as applicable local regulatory requirements and industry best practices.
Do you provide turnkey EPC solutions?
You can reach us through our contact page Yes We offer complete Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) services, including design, procurement, construction, testing, commissioning, and operational support.
Can you customize solutions for specific project requirements?
Yes, Absolutely, Every solution is tailored to the client's technical, operational, and budgetary requirements to ensure optimal performance and long-term value. we can customize our solutions to meet the unique needs of each project; Our team works closely with clients to understand their specific requirements and deliver tailored engineering, procurement, and construction services.
How does a solar power system work?
Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, which can be used immediately, stored in batteries, or integrated with the utility grid depending on the system design.
Can renewable energy systems be integrated with existing diesel generators?
Yes, renewable energy systems can be integrated with existing diesel generators to create hybrid power solutions. This integration allows for a more stable and reliable power supply by leveraging the strengths of both energy sources, We also design hybrid energy solutions that seamlessly integrate solar power, battery storage, and diesel generators to improve reliability, reduce fuel consumption, and lower operating costs.
How much can a solar energy system reduce electricity costs?
Savings depend on energy consumption patterns, system size, local electricity tariffs, and site conditions. Significant long-term reductions in energy costs are commonly achieved.
Do you offer battery energy storage solutions?
Yes. We provide battery energy storage systems (BESS) that enhance energy reliability, provide backup power, and maximize the utilization of renewable energy resources.
What financing options are available?
Financing options vary depending on project size, location, and client requirements. We can assist clients in evaluating suitable financing structures and investment models where applicable.
What is the typical project execution process?
Projects typically follow a structured process including consultation, feasibility assessment, engineering design, procurement, construction, testing, commissioning, and ongoing support.
Do you provide operation and maintenance services?
Yes. We offer preventive and corrective maintenance services, system monitoring, inspections, and technical support to ensure reliable long-term performance.
How can I request a quotation?
You can contact our team with your project requirements, and we will conduct an initial assessment before providing a customized technical and commercial proposal, For information about our service areas and current projects, please visit our about page.
